2. Abstract:
The Bonneville Power Administration funds a long-term effort to study the coastal ecosystems and biological and physical processes affecting salmon recruitment variability in Pacific Northwest waters. Research is currently focused on the Columbia River Plume, an area of high salmonid abundance influenced by human activities. The goal is to understand how biotic and abiotic environmental conditions impact the early marine life history of coho and Chinook salmon, which can help manage higher trophic level predators and ensure sustainable salmon populations in the region.
