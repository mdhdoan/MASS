2. Abstract:
Length frequency distribution is an essential tool for understanding fish population dynamics. It helps biologists identify issues such as year-class failures, growth problems, and high annual mortality rates. Data collected through fisheries sampling activities can serve as a reliable representation of the entire population. This method provides valuable insights into the status and health of fish populations, allowing for informed decision-making and management strategies.
