 1. Keywords: catch-per-unit-effort (CPUE), density-dependent response, downstream dispersal, stratified random sampling, long term resource monitoring
2. Abstract: The method of utilizing catch-per-unit-effort (CPUE) in a stratified random sampling design is essential for long term resource monitoring of fish populations. This approach allows for the evaluation of density-dependent responses by correlating CPUE with variables such as the median time of downstream dispersal from rearing areas. The information gathered through this method provides crucial insights when assessing the status of a population under higher abundance levels in the future, ensuring effective management and conservation strategies.
